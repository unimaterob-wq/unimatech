Featured Product

SUBLUME

R$600,00

Faced with the challenge of cataloging and studying marine life, we created an underwater lighting system that attracts marine species to specific areas, facilitating data collection. They use stainless steel chains to support the system, which is attached at the top by a buoy, and supported at the bottom by a weight. The system is encased in an acrylic box to protect the lights.
